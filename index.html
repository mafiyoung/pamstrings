About Pamstrings

Pam Gazu "Pamstrings" ( born 30 December 1990) is an Nigerian singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. His career began when he met record producers Word and Sound, After signing to Word and Sound Records in July 2020, Pamstrings released his debut extended play, An Introduction to Pamstrings, later that year.
