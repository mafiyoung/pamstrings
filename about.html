Pamstrings

Birth Date

Pam Gazu also known as Pamstrings was born on December 30, 1986 in Jos, a capital of Plateau State, Nigeria.

Career

He attended TCNN School in Jos, and was very enthusiastic about music, He is a multi-talented musician.

More than a singer

He also plays the drums and guitar,and He’s a highly-accomplished pianist player. He learned to play piano as a youngster and originally taught himself to play guitar to impress a girl.

Latest Hits

Pamstrings has rapidly risen to prominence, first in the Nigeria and more recently in Africa. Hits:Ga Ni Na Zo, Kaunar Allah.

Pam Gazu (Pamstrings) was born on 30 December 1986 in Jos, the last of five children (he has two brothers and two sisters). At 9, he began playing the piano and at 14 began learning guitar. He attended TCNN High School in Jos. At 15, he started songwriting and while at TCNN College entered a singing competition and won.

Ga Ni Na zo - Debut Album
Pamstrings's debut album Gani Na Zo was released in Septempber 2016.
Artistry & Influences
Pamstrings possesses the vocal range of a light-lyric soprano that spans 3.5 octaves, and is noted for his high piercing vibrato, breathy tone and emotive delivery.
Personal Life
Pamstrings is close friends with Nigerian singer Neken Chuwang.
